The Latent Space

Technically? I’m driving a statistical engine. I’m feeding keywords into a black box that has crunched the numbers on millions of songs... It’s math. It’s cold, hard probability.

But that’s not what it feels like. It feels like exploration. Wading into that ocean of statistical average and looking for the weird, the broken, the specific.

My job isn't to write the notes anymore. My job is to be the explorer. To take a flashlight into that latent space and find the things that make me feel something. To sift through the "statistically probable" until I find the "emotionally true."
